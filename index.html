About Echelon ’26

The grand event named Echelon ’26 is a dynamic entrepreneurial summit designed to ignite innovation, strategic thinking, and business acumen among students. This multi-event program brings together aspiring entrepreneurs to experience real-world business challenges through engaging competitions.

Make or Break

Startup Decision Making

Make or Break is a strategic business simulation where participants act as startup founders or consultants tasked with rescuing a failing venture.

Quizpreneur

Entrepreneurship Quiz

Quizpreneur is an engaging entrepreneurship-based quiz event designed to test participants’ knowledge of business, startups, economics, marketing, and current affairs.

Behind the Brand

Marketing Analysis

This event tests participants’ ability to analyze real-world marketing strategies of assigned brands. Teams will study a campaign’s success or failure and present their insights. They must also propose innovative solutions, demonstrating strategic thinking and teamwork.

Identity Unleashed

Brand Wars

Rebrand, reposition, and defend your brand identity against aggressive market rivals in a simulated corporate war room.

Decision Table

Crisis Management

Decision Table is a case-based event where teams analyze a business crisis and create a quick, practical solution. The event tests decision-making, teamwork, and problem-solving skills under pressure.

Biz Auction

Strategic Bidding

Biz Auction is an investment simulation where teams bid on companies to build a high-value portfolio within a fixed budget. The event tests financial insight, planning, and competitive thinking.
