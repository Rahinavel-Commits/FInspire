GENERAL RULES

  • All participants must be registered prior to the event. Entry without valid registration will not be permitted.
  • Participants must carry a valid College ID card at all times and produce it for verification when required.
  • Participants are required to report to the venue at least 30 minutes prior to the scheduled time of their event.
  • Registration once completed is non-transferable and non-refundable.
  • Participants must strictly adhere to the time limits allotted for each round/event. Exceeding the time limit may lead to disqualification.
  • Use of mobile phones, smart watches, internet access, or any unfair means during the event is strictly prohibited unless specifically permitted.
  • Participants must maintain discipline, decorum, and professional behavior throughout the event.
  • Any form of malpractice, plagiarism, misrepresentation, or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must bring their own devices, chargers, adapters, and any other required materials for PPT presentations.
  • Decisions taken by the judges and organizing committee shall be final and binding.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to make changes to the event structure, schedule, or rules if necessary.
